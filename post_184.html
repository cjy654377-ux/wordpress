Seoul is the K-Pop capital of the world. The Big 4 agencies — HYBE, SM, JYP, YG — all have visitable locations within the city. Here’s your complete pilgrimage guide.

🎵 The Big 4 K-Pop Tour

For K-Pop fans, visiting the agency buildings is like visiting the Vatican — it’s where the magic happens. Each company has created fan-friendly spaces ranging from full museums to merch stores to cafes. In 2025, K-Pop tourism generated over $1.2 billion for Seoul’s economy.

💡 Pro Strategy: Do all 4 in one day. Start at HYBE (opens 10:30 AM, needs reservation), then SM/JYP/YG are all reachable by subway. Book HYBE tickets 2 weeks in advance — they sell out fast.

🏢 The 4 Must-Visit Locations

Agency Location What’s There Cost
💜 HYBE Yongsan (Line 1) HYBE Insight Museum — interactive exhibits, artist galleries, recording studio experience, merch store 22,000₩ ($16)
💙 SM Seongsu (Line 2) SMTOWN @ Seoul — hologram theater, artist memorabilia, photo zone, cafe Free entry (exhibits vary)
💛 JYP Cheongdam (Bundang Line) JYP building exterior — iconic photo spot. Nearby: JYP-affiliated cafes Free (exterior only)
🖤 YG Hapjeong (Line 2/6) YG building, nearby Teddy’s YG Republique restaurant complex Free exterior / Restaurant prices vary

🎫 HYBE Insight Deep Dive

⏰ Hours: 10:30 AM – 7:30 PM (Last entry 6:30 PM), Closed Mondays
🎫 Booking: Reserve on interpark.com 2+ weeks ahead. 90-minute time slots.
📱 Experience: Interactive music mixing, dance practice room, hologram concerts, original costumes/awards on display
🛍️ Merch Store: Ground floor, no ticket needed. BTS, TXT, LE SSERAFIM, NewJeans exclusive goods
💰 Budget tip: The merch store alone is worth visiting even without museum tickets

🚇 Optimal Day Route

10:30 AM: HYBE Insight (Yongsan) — 90 min museum + 30 min merch store
1:00 PM: Lunch at Yongsan Station food court
2:30 PM: SM TOWN (Seongsu) — Line 1 to Line 2, 30 min
4:30 PM: JYP Building (Cheongdam) — photo op, 15 min
5:30 PM: YG area (Hapjeong) — YG Republique dinner
💰 Total transport: ~6,000₩ ($4.50) all subway

💬 Fan Experience

“HYBE Insight made me cry twice. Seeing BTS’s actual costumes up close, hearing their music through the interactive exhibits, and then spending way too much money at the merch store. Worth every won. The recording studio experience where you mix an actual track is incredible.” — @armypilgrimage

🎶 Every K-Pop fan needs to make this pilgrimage at least once. Seoul’s Big 4 tour is the ultimate fan experience.
